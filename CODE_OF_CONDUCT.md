# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive community.

Expected behavior:

- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating.
- Focus feedback on code, behavior, and outcomes.
- Share context when reporting bugs or regressions.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Doxxing, threats, or intimidation.
- Repeated disruption of discussions or reviews.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to project spaces, including issues, pull requests,
discussions, and other community channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that
violate this policy.

Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from
project participation.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness harmful behavior, open an issue requesting a
private contact channel with minimal public detail.
